Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment necessary! We welcome walk-ins. However, you can call ahead to check wait times, especially during busy periods.

What payment methods do you accept?

Please call us at (718) 846-3871 to confirm our current payment options.

How long does a typical haircut take?

Most haircuts take 20-30 minutes. We're efficient without compromising quality!

Do you accept kids?

Absolutely! We love working with kids and provide patient, friendly service for children of all ages.

What if I'm not sure what style I want?

Our experienced barbers are happy to provide recommendations based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. We'll work with you to find the perfect style.

How busy does it get?

We tend to get busy, especially on weekends, which is a testament to our quality! Call ahead or come during weekday mornings for shorter wait times.
